Overview

This tool uses breadth-first search and various pruning techniques to compress text. Enter in text that you want to compress in the box below. You can choose from the code.org pre-set options or enter your own. Skip the second box for now, only use it if you know what you're doing.

More info

The heuristic ratio determines the aggressiveness of the pruning of the algorithm. The higher the heuristic, the faster the algorithm but the more likely it cuts out good dictionaries.
The iteration ratio determines how long the algorithm is willing to try to find the best solution. 0.3 is default, 0.7 is thorough, and 0.9 is more than enough.
The minimum nodes value will guarantee the heuristic will not leave less than the specified amount of nodes.
The 'deeper segment searching' option adds additional segments to the list of segments, that may or may not help the compression. Use this if you're willing to wait for potentially better results.
